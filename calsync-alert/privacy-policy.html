Privacy Policy for CalSyncAlert

CalSyncAlert (herein called "this app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle user information.

1. Information We Access

This app accesses your iOS Calendar data to provide alarm functionality based on your scheduled events. This access is essential for the core functionality of the app.

Important: All calendar data accessed by this app remains solely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your calendar information to external servers.

2. Personal Information Collection

This app does not collect or store any personal information of users. The application does not gather user data such as names, email addresses, location information, or usage statistics.

3. Calendar Data Usage

Calendar data is used exclusively for:

  • Reading event schedules to set appropriate alarms
  • Displaying event information within the app

This data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted over the internet.

4. Third-Party Data Sharing

This app does not provide user data or calendar information to third parties. No data communication occurs via the Internet.

5. Data Storage

All data generated by the app, including alarm settings, remains solely on your device. We do not maintain any external databases or cloud storage for user data.

6. User Rights

You can revoke calendar access at any time through your iOS Settings. Since we do not collect personal information externally, questions regarding data deletion from our servers do not apply.

7. Security Measures

This app operates only in a local environment on your iOS device and does not transmit data to external servers. Your calendar data is protected by iOS's built-in security features.

8. Children's Privacy

As this app does not collect any personal information externally, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

9. Policy Modifications

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy if deemed necessary. Any updates will be posted on this page.

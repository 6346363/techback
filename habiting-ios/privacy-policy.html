Privacy Policy for Habiting

Habiting (herein called "this app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle user information.

1. Personal Information Collection

This app does not collect or store any personal information of users. The application contains no functionality to gather or retain user data such as names, email addresses, location information, or usage statistics.

2. Third-Party Data Sharing

This app does not provide user data to third parties. No data communication occurs via the Internet. All data generated by the app remains solely on your device.

3. User Rights

Since this app refrains from collecting personal information, questions regarding data deletion, access rights, or data portability do not apply.

4. Security Measures

This app operates only in a local environment on your iOS device and does not transmit data to external servers. Therefore, special security measures related to data transmission are unnecessary.

5. Children's Privacy

As this app does not collect any personal information, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

6. Policy Modifications

While unlikely to change given the app's design, we reserve the right to update this privacy policy if deemed necessary. Any updates will be posted on this page.
